About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm Sander Selfors, a front-end developer student currently based in Oslo. Throughout my first year as a student, I have cultivated a strong passion for web development and design. Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I enjoy creating interactive and visually appealing user experiences. I also have experience working with WordPress, using it as a Headless CMS. Additionally, my design skills, honed with tools like Figma, enable me to transform ideas into aesthetically pleasing and functional designs.


I firmly believe that exceptional user experiences are rooted in good design. Combining my love for design and development, I strive to create websites that not only look visually stunning but also provide intuitive and seamless interactions.


Collaboration and effective communication are crucial in the field of web development. I understand the importance of teamwork and have also worked most of my life with sales in different areas. Beyond coding and design, I find inspiration in traveling and meeting new people, the great outdoors, and spending time with friends and family. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I invite you to explore my projects and follow me on my journey as a front-end developer.


If you have any questions or would like to discuss potential collaborations, please don't hesitate to reach out. I look forward to connecting with you!
