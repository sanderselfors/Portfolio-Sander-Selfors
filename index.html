My Projects

ExpandMedia

This was my first exam at Noroff. The case was to build a blog using Headless CMS to store the content, and using REST API to fetch the data. I chose to create a blog about marketing. I won an award for "Best Student Work" on this website.

BIDIT.

This was the semester-project for my second year. I was tasked with creating an auction website. When a new user joins the website, they are given 1000 credits to use on the site. They can get credits by selling items and use credit by buying items.

Holidaze

My final exam at Noroff. I developed a modern front-end for a new accommodation booking site. The project involved creating a user-friendly interface for customers to book holiday venues and an admin side for managers to register and manage their venues and bookings.

About Me

Welcome to My Portfolio!

I'm Sander Selfors, a front-end developer based in Oslo, recently completing my two years of study at Noroff. Over this time, I've deepened my passion for web development and design, becoming proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and gaining significant experience with React, Tailwind CSS, and working with APIs. My journey has been highlighted by winning the Golden Egg Award for "Best Student Work" for my project "ExpandMedia." I enjoy creating interactive and visually appealing user experiences and have also worked with WordPress as a Headless CMS. My design skills, honed with tools like Figma, enable me to transform ideas into functional and aesthetically pleasing designs.
